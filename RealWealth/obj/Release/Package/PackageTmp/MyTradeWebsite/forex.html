Kinds of Forex Trading

What is Forex Trading ?

The proccess of buying and selling currencies is forex trading. With a daily turnover of a5 trillion, this is the biggest stock market in the world and it includes many individuals and many currencies. You exchange 'coin pairs' because you still purchase one currency using another currency.

What is Forex Market ?

A global online network where traders buy and sell currencies is the foreign exchange market. It is the world's oldest and most liquid stock exchange. Demand and supply describe the exchange rate gaps that, in essence, decide the earnings of traders.

How big is Forex ?

In April 2019, trade in FX market hit a6.6 trillion a day, up from a5.1 trillion three years earlier. FX derivatives rading growth, particularly in FX swaps, outpaced spot trading growth.

Why Forex Traders loose money ?

Their aim is to be successful and get payouts as people reach the foreign exchange market. And in certain situations, by having a certain structure they may adopt and having the market working in their favour, they continue to be efficient. Sadly, however, an ordinary Forex Trader is set to fail because trading is a zero-sum game where another group fails while one group of traders succeeds.
